Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 January 2026

HARSAC (Haryana Space Applications Centre) and GMDA (Gurugram Metropolitan Development Authority) are committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the Attendance App.

Purpose of the App

The Attendance App is an official government application used exclusively for attendance marking and verification of authorized government employees.

Information We Collect

  • Attendance timestamps (check-in and check-out)
  • GPS location data at the time of attendance marking
  • Basic device and application usage information for security and functionality

Location Data Usage

Location data is collected only while the app is in use (foreground) to verify attendance at authorized locations. Location access is essential for the core functionality of attendance validation.

How We Use the Data

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • Attendance verification
  • Internal government reporting
  • Administrative audit purposes

Data is not shared with any external or third-party organizations outside authorized government departments.

Data Storage and Retention

All data is stored securely on government-controlled infrastructure. Attendance records are retained as per applicable government record-retention policies.

Data Deletion and User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting the authorities at the email addresses provided below.

Service Providers

Trusted service providers may be engaged solely for technical support and maintenance. They are bound by confidentiality agreements and cannot use the data for any other purpose.

Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect data. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure.

External Links

The app may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
